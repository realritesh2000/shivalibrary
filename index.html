About Us

More Than Just Books

We provide a modern, comfortable, and silent space optimized for students, professionals, and avid readers. Our library is equipped with high-speed internet, power backup, and comfortable ergonomic seating.

Whether you are preparing for competitive exams or working remotely, our environment fosters focus and productivity.

Our Facilities

Study Environment

A strictly silent zone with soundproofing and ergonomic chairs designed for long study hours.

Safety & Security

24/7 CCTV surveillance and secure entry management to ensure a safe learning space.

Utilities & Power

Uninterrupted power supply with backup generators, RO purified drinking water, and clean washrooms.

Fully Air-Conditioned

Climate-controlled rooms to maintain the perfect temperature for focus in any season.

Learning Resources

Access to daily newspapers (Hindi & English), monthly magazines, and competitive exam materials.

High-Speed WiFi

Leased line fiber optic internet connectivity for seamless online classes and research.

Ergonomic Seating

Comfortable chairs designed for long hours of sitting without fatigue.

Reference Books

Access to a wide collection of daily newspapers, magazines, and reference books.

Vehicle parking facility

A dedicated vehicle parking area designed to make your visit stress‑free. With ample space for both two‑wheelers and four‑wheelers, students and professionals can park securely and focus entirely on their studies.

Elevator Facility

Accessibility is a priority at our Self Study Centre. To ensure that every learner can reach their study space with ease, we provide a modern elevator system within the premises.
